Faqs

This page contains frequently asked questions or concerns our customers may have. From questions concerning shipping to ingredients. Every burning question our loyal customers have can be found answered by scrolling down and viewing the inquiries. We strive to maintain honest sales and the best way to do that is by creating a safe space for customers feedback like you see below. Thank you for choosing Planet Cocoa!

Are your products non-gmo?

Yes! We are proud to serve non-gmo products with clean ingredients that are easy on the kids and not hard to pronounce :)

Are your products vegan?

Are your products gluten free?

While most of our products are not, we do offer gluten free choices for everyone to enjoy.

How long is shipping?

Shipping takes approximately 3-5 business days and is only available in the US

Do your products contain allergens?

Some of our goods do contain nuts or eggs, but rest assured... we will always have it listed.
